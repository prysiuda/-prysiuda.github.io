There are rumors that email marketing is dying or already dead. Such statements grow up in parallel to social media popularity. It is predictable - more and more people join social media life and spend plenty of time there daily. Though, research shows that email is still a powerful marketing tool. Look below and get to know why. Let us compare the number of email and social media users from Statista, a German online portal for statistics. They show the total number of email users in the world crossed 3.8 billion in 2018. It is higher than the total number of social media users worldwide (2.62 billion in 2018).
